Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity

There is no clear relationship that can be drawn between a cities humidity level and its latitude location. Cities from -60 to 80 degrees latitude have all displayed 100% humidity. Few records to note are that the only cities with 0% humidity are between 60- and 80-degrees latitude. For the most part, this graph is a random scatter plot with most points being above 40% humidity.
